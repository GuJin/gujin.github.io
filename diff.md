## What Image Compare does

Image Compare is a browser-based tool for comparing screenshots and images locally. The live tool runs at `diff.gujin.tech`.

Use this page when you want a quick overview, product-page links, or privacy context before opening the tool.

## Where to start

- [Open the live tool](https://diff.gujin.tech/)
- [Read the English product page](https://diff.gujin.tech/image-compare-tool/)
- [Read the Chinese product page](https://diff.gujin.tech/zh/image-compare/)

## What it helps with

- compare two images side by side
- switch between overlay and diff views
- nudge alignment when screenshots do not line up exactly
- zoom and pan around the active comparison
- export the current comparison view

## Why the live tool uses diff.gujin.tech

The live tool uses a short product domain so it is easy to open and share. `gujin.tech` keeps the official overview and support pages for the tool.
